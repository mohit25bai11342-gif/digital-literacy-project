## 📖 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course, where I took on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador. The objective of this project was to understand and promote digital literacy among students.  

In today’s digital world, it is important not only to use technology but also to use it safely, responsibly, and professionally. Through this project, I explored different digital tools and platforms that are essential for academic and professional growth.  

The project consists of five tasks, each focusing on a specific area of digital literacy such as awareness, professional presence, coding platforms, communication, and cyber safety.

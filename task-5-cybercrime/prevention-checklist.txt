                                     Stay Safe Online Checklist   
          
  
   
 General Safety Tips   
• For every account, create a strong, one-of-a-kind password that combines letters, numbers, and symbols.    
• Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for banking apps, social media, and email.    
• In emails or messages, avoid clicking on dubious links or unidentified attachments.    
   
• Always check website URLs for correct spelling and https://.    
• Steer clear of using public Wi-Fi to access critical accounts.    
• Make sure your devices have the most recent antivirus and security updates installed.    
   
   
 Financial / UPI Safety Tips   
• Never give out your bank account information, UPI PIN, or OTP.    
• On UPI apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm, do not accept unidentified "Collect Requests."    
• Before sending money, make sure the recipient's name and number are correct.    
   
• Steer clear of scanning arbitrary QR codes from unidentified sources.    
   
   
 Social Media & Awareness   
• Do not overshare personal information like phone number, address, or college details.    
• Keep your social media accounts private and monitored.    
• Think before posting—your digital footprint matters for future jobs.    
   
 Reporting Cybercrime   
• Report incidents on the National Cyber Crime Portal: National Cyber Crime Portal    
• Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 immediately in case of financial fraud
